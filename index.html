家族
Our Family

A grandmother's recipe, now at your market.

We are the Yamamoto family — a Japanese family who moved to Central Florida carrying one thing above all else: our grandmother's onigiri. Every rice ball is hand-pressed the morning of the market, using short-grain Japanese rice and the same simple techniques passed down through three generations.

In Japan, onigiri is the food of everyday life — packed into school bags, eaten on hiking trails, shared at family picnics. We want it to feel exactly like that here in Winter Garden. Nothing fancy. Just honest, traditional food made with care.
